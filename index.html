About Me

Hi! My name is Alejandra. I'm an aspiring web developer based out of Chicago. My background is in Finance with a BBA from the University of Michigan, Stephen M. Ross School of Business.

I spent the first 6 years of my career in Finance at Target, Illinois Tool Works, and Groupon. It didn't take long for me to realize I would rather create amazing consumer experiences than manage millions of dollars, so I pivoted over to project managing at Groupon.

I work very closely with developers. My technical curiosity has influenced my decision to embark on the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp journey -- so here I am trying to navigate through the world of a developer.

When I am not busy working or learning to code, I like to spend my time with family and friends, boxing or cycling, serving on the boards of non-profit organizations, or mentoring the youth of Chicago.
